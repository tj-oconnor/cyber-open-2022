A Man With A Dream

It all started in 1985 with our founder and CEO, Earnest Benningsly III and his dream of being able to throw away any utensil he used. From those humble beginnings, and a small loan of 60 million dollars, our company has grown to become one of the world's leading providers of single-use plastic cuttlery.
